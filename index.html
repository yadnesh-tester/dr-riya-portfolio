Dedicated to Creating Natural, Lasting Smiles

Dr. Riya Kandge-Khedkar is an Aesthetic Dentist with focused training in smile design and contemporary cosmetic dentistry. She completed her BDS and Fellowship in Aesthetic Dentistry (FDS) from Saveetha Dental College, Chennai — a NIRF-ranked No.1 private dental institution, known for advanced clinical training and research.

Her clinical approach is centered around precision, detailed evaluation, and preservation of natural tooth structure, with an emphasis on achieving balanced, natural-looking, and long-lasting aesthetic results.

With a strong inclination towards digital smile designing and minimally invasive techniques, she focuses on creating smiles that are in harmony with individual facial features.
